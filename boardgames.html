Board Game Recommendations (In No Particular Order)

Settler's of Cataan
Obligatory classic, most people can pick up easily.
Terraforming Mars
Longer game, has engine building elements. Personal favorite, but takes a while.
Spirit Island
Cooporative game, which is rare. Very enjoyable, run around as spirits protecting an island from invaders.
Root
A war game whose different factions play very different games. Much love to the Vagabond.
Twilight Imperium (the remake)
Much like Terraforming Mars, very interesting, but requires more of a time commitment. Like Root, the different factions can play very differently.
